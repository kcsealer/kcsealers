Your concrete deserves more

KC Sealers was built with the intention of helping homeowners preserve the structural integrity and visual appeal of their concrete. Every step in our process is tailored to provide the best possible care for your driveway, patio, walkway, pool deck, or pavers.

Deep Clean

A high powered pressure washer combined with a rotating surface cleaner prepare the concrete for the next step in the application process. This removes any surface-level dirt or grime and opens up the pores of the concrete to bond more cohesively with our contractor-grade sealant.

Hand-Brushed Edges

Once the concrete has dried, it is ready to be sealed. Nobody wants sticky sealant where it doesn't belong, which is why we start all of our jobs with a thorough hand-brushed edge along the entire perimeter of the concrete.

Quality Seal

Once the concrete has dried, it is ready to be sealed. Our pressurized sealant sprayer and signature cross-hatched rolling technique provide the most even coverage possible, minimizing roller marks and providing total protection.
